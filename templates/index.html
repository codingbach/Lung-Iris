Why?

            

                    While the COVID-19 pandemic has put many healthcare systems at the brink of collapes, it has also called for the automation of medical procedures, with AI in medical imaging being the core focus.
            

        


        

            

                    Lung Iris is born out of the need to automate pneumonia detection in critical coronavirus patients, which can pull down diagnosis time from hours to just seconds. This can help lift off burden for medical staff and provide earlier identification for patients which can boost recovery time.
            

            
HOW?

        



        

            
BEYOND THE PANDEMIC

            
Lung Iris is not only a temporary solution to relieve parts of the pandemic. It is a glimpse into the promising future of AI in medical imaging and healthcare
